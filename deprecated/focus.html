Areas of Focus.

In this section I want to talk a bit about what main main interests and areas of focus are. My great passion is programming. I have worked with various programming languages including Python, Java, C, JavaScript, Swift and bash script. The topics that interest me in programming are almost exclusively topics that are in the logic, in the backend of an application. Frontend programming is of little interest to me and has only been used for a few projects to show visually what the application is doing. See for example my scheduling algorithm simulator.

I try to find my path in the wide range of topics that computer science offers. On the one hand, I am very interested in software development in the backend and would like to make this my profession. But also in this area there are of course many topics to focus on. The second topic I am very interested in is statistics, or the application of statistics in computer science. Therefore, I am also thinking about a focus in the Master's program in Data Science. All in all, I am extremely happy to be able to do my internship in 2022 at Bosch, where I combine exactly these areas. At Bosch, I will be a developer implementing various statistical models, mainly for dimension reduction, in the backend of an application for analyzing vehicle data. I hope to have more to say about my path in the future after the internship.

Instead of being somewhat good at too many things I decided to focus on Python and C as well as SQL and MongoDB excessively. Why? That' s what I have summarized here.

Python is my number one programming language that I love to work with and do most of my projects with. I would rate my knowledge of Python as advanced. I love the clarity of the syntax and the variety of applications as well as the many libraries like Django, numpy, pandas and sklearn.
